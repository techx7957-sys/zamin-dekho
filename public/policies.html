About Us

Zamin Dekho is a property assistance platform that helps buyers discover, evaluate, and purchase land based on their preferences.

We work closely with buyers, brokers, and sellers throughout the journey — from property discovery to final deal closure — ensuring a smooth, guided, and transparent experience.

Our platform connects buyers with a network of brokers who assist in identifying suitable properties, arranging site visits, and coordinating with sellers. Buyers typically interact with brokers during the process, and direct interaction with sellers happens primarily at the final stage of the deal.

Our Property-Check Process

To enhance trust and reduce risks, Zamin Dekho follows a structured property-check process before showcasing listings on the platform. This includes:

  • Initial background checks of the property through available documents and broker inputs.
  • On-ground validation such as location verification, photos, videos, and timestamps.
  • Broker-level quality control based on ratings, past performance, and user feedback.
  • Basic screening of property details to identify obvious inconsistencies or red flags.
Important Disclaimer

While these steps help in reducing risks and improving transparency, Zamin Dekho does not guarantee the legal status or future outcomes of any property. Buyers are strongly advised to conduct their own legal due diligence and verification before making a final purchase decision.

Our Role & Mission

Zamin Dekho’s role is limited to providing assistance, coordination, and support. We do not directly handle or control transaction funds between buyers and sellers.

Our mission is to make real estate transactions more transparent, guided, and accessible, while minimizing the chances of fraud or misinformation.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. This policy outlines how Zamin Dekho collects, uses, and encrypts your data to ensure maximum security.

1. Data We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, Email, Phone Number (verified via Twilio/OTP).
  • Location Data (Crucial): We collect real-time GPS coordinates only when you activate the "Secure Deal Room" or use the "Live Property Capture" feature. We do not track your background location.
  • Device Information: IP Address, browser type, and OS for security and anti-fraud monitoring.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is strictly used to facilitate secure property transactions. Phone numbers are shared with brokers only after a site visit is officially scheduled. We never sell your data to third-party marketing agencies.

3. Data Security & Encryption

All chats inside the Secure Deal Room and payment transactions (via Razorpay) are secured using 256-bit SSL encryption. Passwords are cryptographically hashed using bcrypt before being stored in our secure database.

Refund & Cancellation Policy

We understand that real estate decisions require careful thought. Our refund policy is designed to be fair, transparent, and hassle-free based on the type of payment.

1. Premium Verification Fees

Payments made for "Premium Verification" (typically ₹499) are Non-Refundable as they cover immediate background and legal services provided by our team.

2. Escrow Token Bookings

When you pay a Token Booking Amount via our Escrow system after an admin sets the fee, the property is reserved for you. If you change your mind within the first 24 hours of the token payment, you are eligible for a partial refund.

3. Non-Refundable Deductions

If you request a cancellation within the 24-hour window, a fixed sum of ₹10,000 (comprising the Zamin Dekho Platform Fee and the Broker Consultation Fee) will be deducted from your total Token Amount. The remaining balance will be refunded.

4. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund within the 24-hour window, you must initiate the request via the Support Chat Panel located in the "My Bookings" section of your Dashboard. Requests made after 24 hours will result in zero refund.

5. Processing Time

Approved refunds are processed from our Escrow account. It typically takes 5 to 7 business days for the funds to reflect in your original source of payment (UPI/Bank Account) via Razorpay.
