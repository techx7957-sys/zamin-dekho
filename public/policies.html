About Us

Zamin Dekho is a trust-focused property platform designed to help buyers discover, evaluate, and move forward with property decisions in a more transparent and structured way.

We assist users throughout the property journey — from discovery to site visits and final coordination — by connecting buyers with brokers and sellers in an organized and guided manner.

Our platform enables users to explore properties through detailed listings and short-form visual content, making discovery faster and more engaging. Users can access property details, review relevant information, and take the next steps toward visiting or proceeding with a property directly within the platform.

Our Property-Check Process

To improve transparency and reduce risks, Zamin Dekho follows a structured property-check process before listings are showcased. This process may include:

  • Review of available property documents and basic background inputs.
  • On-ground validation such as location checks, photos, videos, and timestamps.
  • Broker-level quality assessment based on past activity, ratings, and user feedback.
  • Screening of property details to identify visible inconsistencies or potential red flags.

We aim to clearly present what has been checked so users can make more informed decisions.

Payments & Fees

In certain cases, users may initiate property-related payments through integrated payment systems within the platform. These transactions are processed through secure third-party payment gateways. Zamin Dekho does not directly hold or control transaction funds.

Zamin Dekho may charge service or facilitation fees for supporting and coordinating transactions. These charges are communicated clearly to the relevant users during the process before any payment is made.

Site Visits & Verification

Users are encouraged to physically visit and independently verify properties before making any financial commitment. Property visits arranged through the platform do not require any upfront payment.

Important Disclaimer

Zamin Dekho’s role is limited to providing assistance, coordination, and improving transparency in the process. We do not guarantee property outcomes, legal status, or future conditions.

Our goal is to reduce uncertainty in real estate transactions by combining structured verification, clear information, and guided support — helping users move forward with greater confidence.

Refund Policy

1. Platform Fees

Platform-related fees, including subscription, listing, verification, and service charges, are non-refundable once services are initiated. Refunds may be considered only in exceptional cases at the discretion of Zamin Dekho.

2. Property Transaction Payment Flow

Buyer payments, including token or advance amounts, are processed through Zamin Dekho’s authorized payment systems such as Razorpay or Cashfree.

After payment, ₹10,000 is deducted as platform service fee and broker visit/service fee.

This ₹10,000 amount is strictly non-refundable under all circumstances.

3. Remaining Amount Handling

The remaining amount is transferred to the seller as per the agreed deal structure.

4. Refund Handling

Zamin Dekho actively assists users by initiating refund requests, managing communication with relevant parties, and supporting dispute resolution. Refund outcomes are determined based on deal terms, transaction conditions, and agreements between involved parties.

Zamin Dekho provides full support in the process but does not guarantee final refund outcomes.

5. Escrow Based Transactions

Where escrow or structured payment systems are used, fund release or refunds are governed by predefined conditions.

6. Refund Timeline

Any approved platform-level refund will be processed within 5 to 7 business days.
